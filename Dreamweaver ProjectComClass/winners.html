the Winners

There have been many teams that have tried to win this competition and to date 79 national teams have participated in the 21 tournaments that have taken place from 1930 to 2018, however only 8 teams have managed to win the trophy.

  1. Brazil: 5 titles (1958, 1962, 1970, 1994, 2002)
  2. Germany: 4 titles (1954, 1974, 1990, 2014)
  3. Italy: 4 titles (1934, 1938, 1982, 2006)
  4. France: 2 titles (1998, 2018)
  5. Argentina: 2 titles (1978, 1986)
  6. Uruguay: 2 titles (1930, 1950)
  7. Spain: 1title (2010)
  8. England: 1 title (1966)
