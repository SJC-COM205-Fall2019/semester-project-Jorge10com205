The FIFA World Cup

The FIFA World Cup is the most watched sporting event in the world and is arguably the most prestigious competition in soccer where international teams of many countries compete to be crowned the world champions. Just like the Olympics the competition takes place every 4 years with the most recent tournament taking place in Russia in 2018 and the next tournament taking place in 2022 in Qatar.
